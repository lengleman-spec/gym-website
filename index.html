About Us

At GymRoid, we’re more than just a gym — we’re a community of fitness enthusiasts dedicated to helping you reach your goals. With state-of-the-art equipment, expert trainers, and 24/7 access, we make staying fit convenient, motivating, and fun. Whether you’re just starting your fitness journey or pushing to the next level, GymRoid is here to support every rep, every workout, and every milestone.

Meet Our Staff

Mark

Mark is a certified personal trainer who’s passionate about helping clients build strength, confidence, and sustainable fitness habits. He specializes in high-intensity interval training (HIIT) and sports-specific training, designing programs that improve performance, agility, and endurance.

Phil

Phil is a dedicated personal trainer who specializes in weight loss and strength and conditioning, helping clients improve their health, mobility, and overall quality of life. His focus is on building strength, balance, and confidence, making fitness approachable and sustainable at any age.

Melissa

Melissa is a compassionate personal trainer who specializes in pre- and post-natal care. She focuses on building strength, confidence, and healthy movement patterns in pregnant women while supporting mothers through safe, effective training before and after pregnancy. With a patient, encouraging approach, Melissa designs programs that prioritize proper form, overall wellness, and long-term health for every stage of life.
